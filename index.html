ABOUT Scarlett


Hi, I’m Scarlett!

I’m a student, athlete and baker from Barrie, Ontario who’s loved combining baking and beauty from a young age. While I complete my studies on my way to opening a café and bakery, I’m continuing to refine my craft, seeking to offer cakes and cupcakes that are decadent and whimsical.

My signature buttercream frostings and shockingly gluten-free options are community favourites, so I hope you’ll allow me the opportunity of adding beauty and taste to your celebrations of the special events and people in your life.
